Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto, Japan, is a city that seamlessly blends ancient tradition with modern charm. As the former imperial capital, Kyoto is home to breathtaking temples, serene Zen gardens, and vibrant shrines, including the famous Fushimi Inari Shrine with its thousands of iconic red torii gates. Visitors can stroll through the historic streets of Gion, where geishas still practice their art, or experience the stunning beauty of the Arashiyama Bamboo Forest. The city is also a paradise for food lovers, offering exquisite kaiseki cuisine and matcha-flavored treats. Whether you're admiring cherry blossoms in spring or fiery maple leaves in autumn, Kyoto provides an unforgettable journey through Japan’s rich cultural heritage.

Hotel: The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto

Banff, Canada

Banff, Canada, is a breathtaking destination nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, offering stunning alpine scenery and outdoor adventures year-round. As part of Banff National Park, the town is surrounded by snow-capped peaks, turquoise glacial lakes, and abundant wildlife, including elk and grizzly bears. Visitors can explore iconic sights like Lake Louise and Moraine Lake, where crystal-clear waters reflect the towering mountains. In the winter, Banff transforms into a world-class skiing and snowboarding destination, while summer invites hikers to scenic trails and hot springs. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Banff’s natural beauty and charming mountain town atmosphere make it a must-visit destination in Canada.

Hotel: Fairmont Banff Springs

Rental Car: SUV from Enterprise

Santorini, Greece

Santorini, Greece, is a breathtaking island in the Aegean Sea, known for its iconic white-washed buildings, and stunning sunsets over the caldera. Formed by a volcanic eruption thousands of years ago, the island’s dramatic cliffs and crystal-clear waters create a postcard-perfect setting. Visitors can explore the charming villages of Oia and Fira, where narrow cobblestone streets lead to incredible sea views and traditional Greek tavernas. Santorini is also famous for its unique black and red sand beaches, as well as its rich history, including the ancient ruins of Akrotiri. Whether you're indulging in fresh Mediterranean cuisine, sailing along the caldera, or simply relaxing with a glass of local wine, Santorini offers an unforgettable Greek island experience.

Hotel: Katikies Hotel

Rental Car: Hertz

Bali, Indonesia

Bali, Indonesia, is a tropical paradise known for its lush landscapes, rich culture, and vibrant spirituality. Often called the "Island of the Gods," Bali offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. Visitors can explore ancient temples like Tanah Lot and Uluwatu, or experience the serenity of the island’s terraced rice fields in Ubud. Bali is also famous for its beaches, where you can surf the waves in Kuta or unwind on the tranquil shores of Nusa Dua. The island's warm hospitality, rich traditions, and wellness offerings make it an ideal destination for relaxation, adventure, and spiritual rejuvenation.

Hotel: Four Seasons Resort Bali

Rental Car: Jeep from Avis

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona, Spain, is a dynamic city that perfectly combines modernist architecture, historic landmarks, and vibrant street life. Famous for the masterpieces of Antoni Gaudí, such as the Sagrada Família and Park Güell, Barcelona is a city that celebrates art and creativity at every corner. The Mediterranean coastline invites visitors to relax on beautiful beaches, while the bustling La Rambla street offers a variety of shops, cafes, and street performances. From the Gothic Quarter’s medieval charm to the lively tapas bars and markets, Barcelona offers an unforgettable experience of culture, cuisine, and architecture.

Hotel: Hotel Arts Barcelona

Rental Car: Luxury Sedan from Sixt

Honolulu, Hawaii

Honolulu, Hawaii, is a tropical haven that offers a perfect mix of natural beauty and modern city amenities. Located on the island of Oahu, Honolulu is home to world-famous Waikiki Beach, where visitors can enjoy surfing, sunbathing, and breathtaking ocean views. The city’s rich history is on display at Pearl Harbor, while outdoor enthusiasts can hike up to Diamond Head for panoramic views of the island. Honolulu also offers a lively cultural scene, with traditional Hawaiian luaus, local markets, and delicious seafood. Whether you're exploring the island’s natural wonders or soaking in the Aloha spirit, Honolulu is an unforgettable destination.

Hotel: Moana Surfrider, A Westin Resort

Rental Car: Convertible from Alamo
