About Us

About us

At Altitude, we believe that the mountains are not just a place, but a way of life. Founded with a passion for outdoor adventure and a love for the winter season, our resort is designed to offer guests an unparalleled experience in the heart of the snow-covered peaks. From our expertly crafted slopes to our cozy accommodations, every detail at Altitude is tailored to help you create lasting memories. Whether you’re skiing, snowboarding, or simply soaking in the stunning views, we are committed to providing a safe, welcoming environment where everyone—from beginners to seasoned pros—can enjoy the thrill of the mountains.

More than just a ski resort, Altitude is a place where adventure and relaxation come together. Our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and personalized experiences for every guest. With world-class amenities, exciting activities, and breathtaking natural beauty, we pride ourselves on offering an unforgettable escape from everyday life. Whether you're visiting for a weekend getaway or a full winter vacation, we strive to make your time at Altitude memorable, offering a perfect balance of adventure and relaxation for the whole family.

Our Journey at Altitude

Altitude Ski Resort has grown over the years, thanks to the dedication of our team and the support of our wonderful guests. Here are some key milestones that have defined our resort:

  1. Founded in 2010 with the vision of creating a world-class ski destination
  2. Opened our first ski lift in 2012, providing access to over 50 acres of slopes
  3. Introduced snowboarding lessons in 2015 to cater to all winter sports enthusiasts
  4. Expanded our resort in 2018 with new luxury lodges and a full-service spa
  5. Achieved sustainability certification in 2020, focusing on eco-friendly practices

We're proud of our accomplishments and look forward to more exciting developments at Altitude!
