Important Ski Terms at Altitude

At Altitude, we want to make sure you're familiar with the key terms that will enhance your experience on the slopes. Here are some important terms to know:

Powder Snow
Fresh, untouched snow that has not been compacted by skiers or snowboarders, offering a soft and smooth ride.
Apres-Ski
Social activities and entertainment after a day of skiing or snowboarding, often enjoyed at a lodge or bar with drinks and music.
Black Diamond
A challenging ski slope that is marked for advanced skiers, offering steeper gradients and more difficult terrain.

These are just a few of the terms you'll encounter at Altitude—get ready to hit the slopes like a pro!
